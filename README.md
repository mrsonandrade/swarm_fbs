## A Self-Adaptive Swarm of Floating Breakwaters via Particle Swarm Optimization, RRT, and Reinforcement Learning

#### Description:
In the animation below, the swarm of floating breakwaters (colored rectangle) is under the incoming wave actions. The wave direction is 205 degrees, and the wave period is 4 seconds. The black crosses indicate the available positions that the breakwaters may anchor. Thus, after running the optimization using PSO, a better formation will be computed and, in the present case, all breakwaters will be repositioned, except the green one, which uses reinforcement learning and RRT to navigate to its better position. The choice to navigate only one breakwater was made only to demonstrate the feasibility of the proposed solution, minimizing the length of the simulation.
